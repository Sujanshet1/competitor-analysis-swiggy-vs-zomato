# SWOT Analysis

## Swiggy

### Strengths
- Fast delivery
- Frequent discounts
- Wide restaurant network

### Weaknesses
- Higher delivery charges
- UI less modern

### Opportunities
- Improve personalization
- Better loyalty rewards

### Threats
- Strong competition from Zomato

---

## Zomato

### Strengths
- Excellent UI
- Accurate live tracking
- Strong restaurant discovery

### Weaknesses
- Customer support complaints
- Pricing concerns

### Opportunities
- Improve complaint resolution
- Transparent pricing

### Threats
- Swiggy's faster delivery
